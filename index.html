IBM AI(Artificial Intelligence)

WHAT?

*Artificial Intelligence systems are able to simulate human intelligence and thought processes.

* To be considered Artificial Intelligence a system must be capable of ...

  • Understanding
  • Reasoning
  • Learning
  • Interacting

There are 3 types of AI ...

  • Narrow Intelligence - something that has one job and does it very well eg. user using voice to search for something that they know of / hammer used for one purpose
  • Broad Artificial Intelligence - something that learns and makes decisions from the data that it is given eg. self driving cars
  • General Artificial Intelligence - something that learns and makes decisions based on a human like system eg. robots(idk cant think of a better example)...
